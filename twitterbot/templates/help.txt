Hello, I'm a Twitter bot! I can watch twitter users and post their tweets in this channel. I can even notify you if a tweet comes from a user with more than a certain number of followers.

@{{ botname }} follow <twitter-username>
      Follow tweets related to this twitter user.

@{{ botname }} unfollow <twitter-username>
      Stop following this twitter user.

@{{ botname }} following
      List all twitter users followed in current channel.

@{{ botname }} mention <number>
      I'll mention you whenever a tweet appears from an owner with more than <number> followers

@{{ botname }} mention
      I'll tell you what you've current set above
 
@{{ botname }} mention stop
      I'll stop notifiying you when new tweets appear
